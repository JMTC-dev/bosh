About Our Mate, Tyler

For those of you who know Tyler, you know he's a great guy. He's smart, works hard at his IT job at Ashurst, and is always the first one to help you out if you're stuck on something. He's just a genuine, good bloke.

He's the kind of guy who's not just a colleague but a real friend. He loves a good chat over coffee, a walk at lunchtime to find a good feed, and is always up for a games night online. He's just a normal young guy starting his career, and he's one of the best people we know.

Tyler's Toughest Fight

Recently, Tyler got some really bad news: a cancer diagnosis. It came out of nowhere and has been a massive shock. He's facing a huge battle with treatments and everything that comes with it.

He's being incredibly brave, but this is tough. The last thing he should be worrying about is money for medical bills or rent while he's sick. That's why we're doing this. We want to take that stress away so he can just focus on getting better.
